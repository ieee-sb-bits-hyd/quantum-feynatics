Fest at a glance

Hosted by the Quantum Feynatics Group of IEEE BITS Hyderabad in collaboration with IBM Quantum, Get ready to dive into the World of Quantum at QuantumVerse Vol. 3: Qiskit Fall Fest 2025, a unique three-day event where cutting-edge quantum computing meets hands-on learning. This immersive workshop and hackathon will explore the latest advancements in the Era of Quantum Utility—where practical quantum applications are becoming a reality.

Century of Quantum !

Celebrating 100 years of quantum : From theory to technology, from curiosity to computation - Step into the future with us at Qiskit Fall Fest!

Organizers

Quantum Feynatics Group is dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of quantum technologies. Through events, workshops, and seminars, we aim to make quantum knowledge accessible while building a vibrant community of learners and innovators.

IBM Quantum is a global leader in advancing quantum computing, developing hardware, software, and tools to make quantum technology accessible worldwide. Through the Qiskit Fall Fest, IBM collaborates with student communities to spark learning, innovation, and hands-on exploration of quantum computing.

Timeline

  1. Day 1

    We will start off with Basics of Quantum Computing, Python and Qiskit, then move on to the Qiskit SDK. A "Lab 0" notebook will be given to get you familiar with the basics in Python and Qiskit.

  2. Day 2

    Continuation to Qiskit SDK and QUantum Entanglement. We will also have an introduction into Quantum Error Correction. Notebooks will be given to test how much you have learned.

  3. Day 3

    Completion of Quantum Error Correction, a panel discussion with experts in the field, a keynote speaker session, and a Hackathon to test your skills and win exciting prizes.
